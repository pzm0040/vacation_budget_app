User Guide

  • To create a vacation budget, you must first create an account. Once you are logged in, select the ‘Create a Vacation Budget’ button on the home page.
  • Enter all relevant information into the text fields. If you enter an invalid value, you will not be able to submit the vacation budget form. Examples of invalid values include vacation dates in the past and negative dollar amounts for costs.
  • If you do not know a cost yet, submit the default dollar amount of 0 in any of the expense fields. You will be able to modify your budget later to update expenses.
  • When you are finished just click ‘Submit’. You will be taken to a list of Vacations where you will have the option to view budget details, modify vacation, or delete the vacation.

This is the landing page where you can view all your planned vacations.

  • Vacation Budget: This will show your calculated vacation budget (see Vacation Budget Detail Page below).
  • Modify Vacation: Use this page to update any part of your vacation budget. This could include your vacation date or projected expenses.
  • Delete Vacation This will delete your Vacation Budget. Please be aware that a deleted budget cannot be re-activated.

This page calculates everything needed for your vacation budget. Navigate to this page by selecting the ‘Vacation Budget’ icon from ‘My Budget Home’.

  • Vacation Details
    • Countdown: Number of days/months until your vacation.
    • Duration of Trip: Number of days on vacation. Keep this in mind for daily expense projections.
  • Vacation Expenses
    • Total Flight Cost: This can either be the total amount submitted in the form or the total amount submitted multiplied by the ‘Number in Party’ if the option is selected.
    • Total Hotel Cost: This can either be the total amount submitted in the form or the total amount submitted multiplied by the duration of trip (days) if the ‘Hotel per day’ option is selected.
    • Total Food Cost: This can either be the total amount submitted in the form or the total amount submitted multiplied by ‘Number in Party’ and duration of trip (days) if the ‘Food per Person per Day’’ option is selected.
    • Total Activity Cost: This can either be the total amount submitted in the form or the total amount submitted multiplied by ‘Number in Party’ and duration of trip (days) if the ‘Food per Person per Day’’ option is selected.
    • Total Vacation Cost: This will total all sections mentioned above for ‘Vacation Expenses’
  • Savings Goal
    • Monthly Savings Required: This takes your total projected vacation cost and divides it by the number of months remaining before your vacation. This amount should be saved each month to reach your savings goal. Vacations are more fun when you are debt free! We recommend setting up automated transfers to your savings account in this amount. You can then reimburse yourself from savings for expenses as you pay for them.
  • Total Savings
    • Total Savings: This can be adjusted by clicking ‘Modify Vacation’ in ‘My Budget Home Page’. This should reflect the total amount you’ve saved for your vacation.
    • Savings Progress: This divides your ‘Total Savings’ by the ‘Total Vacation Cost’. The goal is to be at 100% by the time you leave for vacation.
